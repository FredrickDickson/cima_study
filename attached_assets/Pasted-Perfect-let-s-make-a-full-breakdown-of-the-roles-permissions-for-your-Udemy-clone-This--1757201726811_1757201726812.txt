# 🎓 Udemy Clone – Roles & Permissions Breakdown

## 🏆 1. **Super Admin (Owner)**

**Full control of the platform.**

* 👤 **User Management**

  * Create, edit, suspend, or delete any user (instructors, students, admins).
  * Approve/reject instructor applications.
* 📚 **Course Management**

  * Approve/reject courses before publishing.
  * Edit or remove any course on the platform.
  * Manage categories, tags, and course languages.
* 💳 **Finance**

  * Manage platform revenue split (e.g., 30% platform, 70% instructor).
  * View all transactions & payouts.
  * Handle refunds and disputes.
* ⚙️ **Platform Settings**

  * Configure branding (logo, colors, landing page).
  * Set global platform rules (pricing tiers, promotions, coupons).
  * Manage integrations (Paystack, email service, analytics).
* 📊 **Analytics & Reporting**

  * View full analytics (sales, enrollments, engagement, top instructors).

---

## 👨‍💻 2. **Admin / Moderator**

**Helps manage the platform under the Super Admin.**

* Approve/reject new courses (if delegated by Super Admin).
* Review course updates submitted by instructors.
* Moderate content (check if courses violate rules).
* Manage support tickets from students/instructors.
* Limited analytics (only course quality & engagement).
* Cannot change financial settings or revenue split.

---

## 👩‍🏫 3. **Instructor (Course Creator)**

**Manages their own courses only.**

* 📚 **Course Management**

  * Create, edit, and delete their own courses.
  * Add sections, lectures, quizzes, PDFs, and assignments.
  * Upload course thumbnail, promo video, and lecture videos.
  * Save as draft or submit for approval.
* 💰 **Earnings**

  * Set course pricing (free or paid).
  * View student enrollments & course revenue.
  * Request payouts (Paystack withdrawal).
* 📊 **Student Interaction**

  * Answer student Q\&A.
  * Respond to reviews.
  * Send announcements/messages to students (if allowed).

---

## 👨‍🎓 4. **Student (Learner)**

**Consumes content and engages with courses.**

* 🔎 **Course Access**

  * Browse/search/filter courses.
  * View course landing page (description, reviews, instructor info).
  * Enroll in free or paid courses.
* 🎬 **Learning Experience**

  * Watch lecture videos, read materials, take quizzes.
  * Track progress within the course.
  * Resume where they left off.
* ⭐ **Engagement**

  * Leave ratings & reviews.
  * Ask questions in Q\&A (if available).
  * Download resources (if instructor provides).
* 🎓 **Completion**

  * Receive certificate of completion (optional).
